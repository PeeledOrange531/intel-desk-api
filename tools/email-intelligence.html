What This Tool Does

An email address is one of the most powerful identifiers in an OSINT investigation — most people use the same email to register across dozens of platforms. This tool runs Holehe, a trusted open-source intelligence tool, against any email address to check whether an account exists across 120+ platforms including Instagram, Twitter, Spotify, Adobe, Patreon, GitHub, Reddit, and many more. It uses each platform's password-reset flow to check for account existence without alerting the target and without requiring any login or API key. Results stream live into the terminal as each check completes. Found accounts include any additional intelligence returned by the platform — partial recovery email addresses, partial phone numbers, and account metadata. Used by journalists verifying source identities, fraud investigators tracing digital footprints, HR professionals conducting background screening, and security researchers mapping attack surfaces.
