What This Tool Does

Every PDF and Word document carries a hidden layer of metadata that most authors don't know exists — and almost never think to remove. This free document metadata extractor surfaces that hidden layer instantly. Upload any PDF or DOCX file and this tool will reveal the original author name, document creator and producer software, creation and last-modified timestamps, total edit time, revision count, and — for Word documents — a full revision history timeline showing every author who touched the document, when, and in what order. It will also flag forensic anomalies: creation dates that postdate modification dates, author name mismatches across revisions, metadata that has been partially scrubbed, and software fingerprints that reveal what platform the document was actually created on — regardless of what the sender claims. For journalists authenticating leaked documents, legal professionals verifying chain of custody, fraud investigators, and OSINT researchers, document metadata is one of the most overlooked and revealing sources of intelligence available. All processing is performed entirely in your browser — no file is ever transmitted to a server.
