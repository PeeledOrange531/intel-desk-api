What This Tool Does

Every webpage contains a wealth of intelligence embedded in its HTML that isn't visible on the surface. This tool fetches any URL server-side and extracts all links (internal and external), email addresses, phone numbers, and social media handles found in the page source. Results are displayed in filterable, sortable tabs with one-click copy for individual items or bulk export of entire categories. Used by journalists mapping a website's network of links, investigators harvesting contact information, OSINT researchers identifying social media presences, SEO analysts auditing outbound links, and security researchers profiling web infrastructure.
