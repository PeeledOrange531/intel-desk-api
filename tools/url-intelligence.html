URL Intelligence

What This Tool Does

Every URL carries far more information than its surface appearance suggests — and visiting it directly exposes you to tracking pixels, fingerprinting scripts, and potential malware. This tool inspects any URL safely without you visiting it. It follows the full redirect chain step by step, revealing every intermediate destination before reaching the final URL — critical for exposing shortened links, affiliate redirects, and deceptive phishing chains. It returns the final destination domain, HTTP status codes, server technology, response time, and a full SSL certificate inspection including issuer, validity window, and Subject Alternative Names. A screenshot preview shows what the page actually looks like. One-click links open the URL against VirusTotal, URLScan.io, Google Safe Browsing, URLhaus, and Sucuri for threat analysis. Used by journalists verifying source links, investigators tracing phishing infrastructure, security researchers analysing malicious links, and anyone who receives a suspicious link.
