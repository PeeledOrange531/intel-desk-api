URL Intelligence

A suite of free tools for inspecting URLs without visiting them. Reveal redirect chains, expand shortened links, decode QR codes, batch-analyze hundreds of URLs, and score them for safety — all server-side.

What These Tools Do

Every URL carries far more information than its surface appearance suggests — and visiting it directly exposes you to tracking pixels, fingerprinting scripts, and potential malware. This suite inspects URLs safely without you visiting them. The URL Expander & Safety Checker follows the full redirect chain step by step, revealing every intermediate destination before reaching the final URL — critical for exposing shortened links, affiliate redirects, and deceptive phishing chains. It returns the final destination domain, HTTP status codes, server technology, response time, and a full SSL certificate inspection including issuer, validity window, and Subject Alternative Names. A screenshot preview shows what the page actually looks like. Every URL is automatically scored on a 0-100 safety scale using domain age, lookalike detection, suspicious TLD heuristics, URL shortener detection, IDN homograph attacks, and known-malware databases. Batch mode processes up to 100 URLs in parallel for verifying lists from phishing reports. QR code mode decodes any QR image entirely in your browser then runs the same inspection. Used by journalists verifying source links, investigators tracing phishing infrastructure, security researchers analysing malicious campaigns, and anyone who receives a suspicious link.
