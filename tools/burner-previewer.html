Burner Link Previewer

See exactly what's on a page without ever visiting it. Full-page screenshot plus extracted page content, embedded forms, and outbound links — all rendered safely on our server.

What This Tool Does

Sometimes you need to see what's on a webpage without giving up your IP address, your fingerprint, or risking the page itself. The Burner Link Previewer fetches and renders any URL on our server, then returns it to you as a full-page scrollable screenshot plus a complete extraction of the page's content. You see the page title, meta description, and Open Graph card exactly as it would appear in a social media preview. You see all headings (H1, H2, H3) in document order, a 1500-character body excerpt, every embedded form with its action URL and field types — with password forms flagged in red as a phishing indicator — and every outbound link with its destination host. Paste a suspicious email or text message and the tool extracts every URL it contains for you to inspect one by one. The link is fetched once on our server using a generic browser user-agent. Your browser never opens the URL. Used by journalists previewing sources, security researchers analysing phishing pages, investigators reviewing suspicious links, and anyone who received a sketchy text and wants to know what's on the other end.
