What This Tool Does

Telegram is one of the most significant platforms for intelligence gathering — used by threat actors, activist groups, news channels, and disinformation networks. This tool provides two modes of investigation. Channel Lookup extracts detailed metadata from any public Telegram channel or group by username — including subscriber count, description, creation date, recent posts, and media. Channel Search links directly to every major Telegram search engine (TGStat, Telemetr, Lyzem, TelegramDB) pre-populated with your query. The Archive function saves channel snapshots locally for longitudinal tracking and comparison. Used by journalists monitoring extremist channels, analysts tracking disinformation campaigns, security researchers mapping threat actor infrastructure, and OSINT investigators building social network graphs.
