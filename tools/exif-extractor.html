What This Tool Does

Every photograph taken on a modern camera or smartphone contains a hidden layer of data known as EXIF metadata — and most people have no idea it exists. This free EXIF data extractor reads that hidden layer instantly. Drop any JPEG, TIFF, or HEIC image and this tool will surface the camera make and model, lens information, shutter speed, aperture, and ISO, exact date and time the photo was taken, software used to edit it, and — critically — precise GPS coordinates if location data was embedded. Those coordinates are plotted on a live interactive map, pinpointing exactly where in the world the photo was captured. For journalists verifying the provenance of images, OSINT investigators geolocating a source, legal professionals authenticating evidence, or anyone verifying whether an image is what it claims to be, EXIF data is one of the most powerful and overlooked sources of ground truth available. All analysis happens entirely in your browser — no image is ever uploaded to a server.
