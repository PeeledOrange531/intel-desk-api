What This Tool Does

Time is one of the most critical — and most frequently misrepresented — elements of any investigation. This tool converts any date and time across every major world timezone simultaneously. Enter a source time and location, and instantly see the equivalent local time in every city you're tracking. Build a custom world clock with only the timezones that matter to your investigation, identify whether a claimed timestamp is plausible given business hours or local conditions, calculate exact time differences between locations, and visualize overlap windows for cross-timezone coordination. Used by journalists coordinating across bureaus, investigators correlating event timestamps from different countries, fraud analysts verifying alibi timelines, OSINT researchers cross-referencing social media posts, and security teams tracking incidents across regions.
