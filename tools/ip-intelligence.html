IP Intelligence

What This Tool Does

Every device connected to the internet has an IP address — and that address carries a wealth of intelligence that most people never think to examine. This free IP intelligence tool performs a comprehensive lookup on any IPv4 or IPv6 address, revealing its geographic location (country, region, city, and coordinates), Internet Service Provider, Autonomous System Number (ASN) and organization, timezone, connection type, and threat indicators including whether the address is associated with a VPN, proxy, Tor exit node, hosting provider, or known malicious activity. It also includes one-click links to cross-reference the IP against additional intelligence sources including Shodan, VirusTotal, AbuseIPDB, and BGP.he. Used by security researchers investigating suspicious traffic, journalists tracing source locations, fraud analysts verifying user locations, network engineers diagnosing connectivity, and OSINT investigators building target profiles. You can also look up your own public IP address instantly.
