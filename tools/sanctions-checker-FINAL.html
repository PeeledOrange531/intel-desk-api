Sanctions List Cross-Checker

What This Tool Does

Sanctions screening is a legal requirement in finance, trade, and professional services — and a critical step in any serious due diligence process. This free sanctions list checker searches four major government watchlists simultaneously: the OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list maintained by the US Treasury, the UN Security Council Consolidated Sanctions List, the EU Consolidated Sanctions List, and the UK Sanctions List (FCDO) maintained by HM Treasury. Enter any individual name, entity, vessel, or organization and this tool will scan all four lists in real time, returning any matches with their designation type, listed nationality, sanctioning program, and listing date. Used by compliance officers, investigative journalists, legal professionals conducting due diligence, financial institutions, and OSINT researchers who need a fast, free, cross-jurisdictional sanctions screen without a paid API subscription. Lists are fetched live from official government sources and cached for six hours.
